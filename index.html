Common questions

Whats included in cleanings? Fairy provides easy-to-use checklists. Need something special? Just ask.
What happens after I book? We'll text you a confirmation and your housekeeper's contact info so you can start chatting.
Do I have to be home for my first cleaning? Housekeepers love meeting their customers, but it’s up to you.
Are supplies provided? Housekeepers bring basic cleaning supplies. Have specific preferences? Just let your housekeeper know.
When is my credit card charged? You're only charged after your first cleaning. 100% money-back guaranteed.
Where do I leave my keys? Leave your keys with your doorman or put them in a lockbox (just make sure to let your housekeeper know where they are located).
What happens if I want to change my Primary Housekeeper? Request a different Primary Housekeeper at any time.
What happens if I go out of town? Pause and continue your service as needed.
